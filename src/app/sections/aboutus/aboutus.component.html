About Us

Gardening Techs brings landscaping and excavation experience and horticultural knowledge to your residential, commercial, and industrial outdoor space. If it’s high-quality custom-landscape creation you want, Gardening Techs will deliver. As a licensed excavator operator and horticulturist, Gardening Techs understands both the art and science required to create an exterior space that not only looks great but functions well.

Along with the team, we works with you to plan and prepare your outdoor space before completing the construction and installation phase – having our input and design advice from the start ensures you receive a better result. Whether we are repairing or redoing an existing space or starting with a blank canvas, we create exteriors based on your needs and want to make sure we overcome any landscaping obstacles, such as site slopes, ground conditions, water, wind, sun, and available space.
